About me

I’m a filmmaker with experience in writing and directing, focused on creating stories that connect with audiences emotionally. Since starting in 2018 as an assistant director, I’ve worked on a range of projects as assistant and associate director, including documentaries, short films, ad films, and music videos. I’ve also had the chance to work as an assistant cinematographer and gain experience in various departments like sound and art. These diverse experiences have shaped my approach to filmmaking, and I believe in the power of storytelling to inspire and engage, and I aim to bring that vision into every project I take on.
